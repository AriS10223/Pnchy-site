## What Is Pnchy?

Pnchy is a loyalty aggregator and community platform built for independent local businesses — coffee shops, comic book stores, restaurants, barbershops, and fitness studios with three or fewer locations.

It connects consumers and merchants through three core mechanics:

- **Digital Punch Cards** — Consumers generate a single-use QR code on their phone. Merchants scan it to stamp a digital loyalty card. No hardware required. Works in under 3 seconds.
- **Density Drops** — A group reward that activates when enough verified Pnchy users gather near a merchant within a time window. Push notifications fire as the threshold approaches. A flash reward for everyone who shows up.
- **Owner of the Block** — A daily-updated competitive leaderboard for merchants, ranked by category within their neighborhood (e.g., #1 coffee shop in downtown State College) based on verified foot traffic.

Pnchy is **hardware-free** and **POS-agnostic** — it works above any payment system without requiring new equipment or switching processors.

## Who It's For

**Consumers:** Penn State students and State College residents who want to discover and support local independent businesses and earn real rewards for being regulars. One app for all the spots downtown — not a separate loyalty card for every place.

**Merchants:** Independent business owners who have never had real customer visit data and can't afford or don't need enterprise loyalty tools like Paytronix. Starts free. Paid tiers from $29/month.

---

## The Town-Gown Thesis

State College, PA has 47,000 enrolled Penn State students and a permanent population of ~42,000 living in the same walkable downtown. No existing loyalty platform is designed for a market where students and local residents share the same independent businesses. Pnchy is.

The Density Drop mechanic is especially powerful here: when a comic shop runs a weekend afternoon Drop, students who found it on the map and locals who have been going for years show up together. That's a community event, not a promotion.
